Common Questions

Answers to questions practice owners ask before getting started.

We already get reports from our practice management software. Why do we need this?
It's like the difference between a thermometer and a doctor. Your software tells you what happened. We tell you why it happened and what to do. Your reports show 15% no-shows. We tell you it's Tuesday afternoons with patients under 35, and here's how to fix it. We take your data and turn it into decisions.
We don't have time for a big implementation. How does this work?
We handle everything. Implementation takes 3 weeks from start to finish. After that, you spend 15 minutes a month reviewing your dashboards and 30 minutes on strategy calls. No manual data entry, no spreadsheets. We run everything in the background so you can focus on your practice.
Our data is messy. Are we ready for this?
Perfect. Messy data is our job. We've worked with 1,000-chair health systems and solo practitioners. Your software has the data. Most practices just don't have anyone to make sense of it. We spot data-quality issues as we work and help you fix them while delivering clean insights.
What happens in the free consultation?
We spend 30 minutes learning about your practice: what software you use, what reports you currently get, and what questions you wish you could answer. We'll identify 2-3 specific insights you're missing and explain how we'd help. If it's a good fit, we'll discuss next steps. If not, we'll point you in the right direction. No sales pressure.
What if we need to cancel?
No long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee—if you don't see clear value in the first month, we'll refund your first month. We only keep clients who are getting results.
How is our patient data kept secure?
We follow strict HIPAA-compliance protocols. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We sign Business Associate Agreements (BAA) with every client. Your patient information never leaves our secure healthcare cloud. We can send you our full security documentation if you need it for your internal review.
Can you work with med spas and aesthetic practices?
Yes. We work with dental practices, medical clinics, and med spas. The data challenges are similar: understanding client retention, treatment profitability, and revenue optimization. We've built analytics for wellness and aesthetic practices in addition to traditional healthcare.
Do you do AI or predictive analytics?
Yes. Beyond dashboards and reports, we build custom AI solutions for patient engagement, no-show prediction, and treatment acceptance forecasting. These projects typically start at $10,000 and include strategy, development, and training. We can discuss AI opportunities in your consultation.
